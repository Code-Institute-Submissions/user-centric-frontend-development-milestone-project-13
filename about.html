What We Are

Our Aim

Skills & Scales Hypnotherapy firstly focuses on the Skills you need to achieve your goals. We not only use hypnotherapy to strengthen what you already know, we also help you figure out how best for you personally to acquire future skills.

Secondly, is the Scales. We are about delivering balanced and measureable results. To provide a practical, solutions-focused service.

Why We Are

Our Origin

Skills & Scales was initially conceived to provide hypnotherapy solutions for people who experience anxiety when speaking English as a foriegn language. Our scope later expanded to cover a wide range of disciplines.

How We Are

Our Philosophy

Skills & Scales believes strongly in the importance of feeling connected. Whether you're learning a language, getting your degree in mathematics, writing a novel or playing in an international golf tournament - whatever you are persuing, it tends to all boil down to connection. We want to communicate with people, understand what other people have understood or achieve to impress others and gain their respect. Sometimes it's to connect with people, sometimes it's to feel connected to the universe, other times it's about connecting with ourselves. That feeling of being connected is critical to being human. Which is why all those goals are so important, not for what they are, but for what they make you personally feel. Those goals are worth getting support for and worth supporting.
